Your situation:

As a EU citizen you have the broadest choice of how to do your solopreneur business in Bulgaria. But should you not be an EU citizen not all is lost! Just let us know so we can recommend the best course of action.

You're on the move, you're seeking a new home for your business. Bulgaria is an excellent choice for that - but are you ready yet to commit yourself? Let us know what your thinking is about Bulgaria as the home for your business so we can select the best option for setting it up. If you're not sure yet, that's ok, too. Even then there are ways to benefit from the Bulgarian tax system.

To recommend the best membership plan for you it's helpful to know in what way your business is cooperating with subcontractors. Are you delegating tasks to others who then invoice you? This is especially relevant if such subcontractor costs eat up a substantial part of your revenue or are fluctuating quite a bit over the year.

If you already have or plan to have employees working in your business that's important to know for selecting the best option to set up your business. If you see employees in the present or near future being relevant to you, check this option.

Dealing with physical goods makes a huge difference for setting up your business in Bulgaria. Do you keep a stock around? You should also check this option if your inventory is kept at an Amazon warehouse or the like. In contrast, if you're selling just digital goods or print-on demand books then leave this option unchecked.
Should your business hold or accumulate considerable intellectual property (IP), consider that a form of inventory, too.

If you fear you could be made liable for some damage caused by you with some client then check this option. We want to make sure this does not endanger your personal wealth now or in the future.

Don't worry, we don't want to now exactly how much money you make with your business, but a rough idea would be helpful to select the best course of action for getting your business benefit from Bulgaria as a host country. Nomad Nation offers options if you don't earn much (yet) - or already have larger amounts of money rolling in each month.

Don't worry, you don't need to dive into your bookkeeping records and figure out exactly how many expenses you incurr each month or year. But a rough idea would really help us to recommend one of our membership plans. The Bulgarian law is offering a very attractive option if the right criteria are met; maybe that's the one for you...
